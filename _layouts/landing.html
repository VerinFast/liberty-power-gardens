What We Deliver

Divert Food Waste

We keep food waste out of landfills through onsite community-scale processing, turning a burden into a resource.

Reduce Emissions

Onsite processing eliminates vehicle emissions from waste hauling. Captured methane is repurposed instead of released.

Lower Utility Bills

Harvested biogas powers onsite generators that provide electricity and heat back into the surrounding neighborhood.

Energy Independence

Renewable energy generated locally means communities are less dependent on centralized infrastructure and volatile energy markets.

Our Approach

Unlike other food waste companies, our aim is neighborhood-by-neighborhood beautifying. Our sites become community gardens that pump potable water and renewable resources back into the local ecosystems they serve.
